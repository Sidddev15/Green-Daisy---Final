Experience the Richness of Assam Tea with Green Daisy.

Assam, nestled in the northeastern region of India, is home to some of the world's finest tea gardens. The region's unique geographical location, fertile soil, and tropical climate create the perfect conditions for growing tea. At Green Daisy, we take pride in sourcing our tea leaves directly from these lush tea estates, ensuring that only the highest quality tea makes it to your cup.
Here are some reasons why Assam tea is truly exceptional:

  • Bold and Robust Flavors
  • High Caffeine Content
  • Health Benefits
  • Versatility

So, sip on a cup of Green Daisy's Assam tea and experience the richness of northeastern India's tea culture in every sip.
